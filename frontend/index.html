Why check for data leaks?

Cybercrime is on the rise, and stolen credentials are sold on the dark web every day. Identity theft, financial fraud, and unauthorized account access are just a few of the risks. By quickly checking if your data is exposed, you take the first step toward safeguarding your privacy

Prevent Identity Theft

Hackers can use your personal information to impersonate you, apply for loans, or access your private accounts.

Stay Ahead of Hackers

Proactively secure your accounts before criminals exploit leaked passwords or personal details

Protect Your Reputation

Compromised social media or email accounts can damage your online presence and relationships.

Peace of mind

Knowing your data status helps you sleep better at night, confident you’ve taken measures to protect yourself.

How LeakNix works?

Enter Your Details. Provide the email, username, or phone number you want to check.

We Scan Multiple Databases. Our system combs through breached data sources, dark web listings, and other repositories of stolen credentials.

Receive a Comprehensive Report. Instantly see if your details appear in any known breaches, along with important information like which services were affected.

Get Personalized Security Tips. Based on your report, we’ll guide you on securing your accounts, updating passwords, and protecting your personal information.

What you get with your LeakNix report

Breach Summary. A clear list of all incidents where your
data may have been compromised.

Exposure Details. Insights into the type of data leaked (passwords, phone numbers, addresses, etc.).

Continuous monitoring. Opt-in for alerts whenever we detect new exposures linked to your details.

Security recommendations. Actionable steps to strengthen your accounts,
create safer passwords, and enable two-factor authentication.
